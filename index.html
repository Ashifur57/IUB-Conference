About our Conference

Independent University, Bangladesh (Bengali: ইন্ডিপেন্ডেন্ট বিশ্ববিদ্যালয়) or IUB is a private university in Bangladesh. It is located in Bashundhara Residential Area of Dhaka, Bangladesh. It was established in 1993 under the Private University Act, 1992.[1][2] IUB has an enrollment of 10,000 students, 11,556 alumni, and 401 faculty members (of which 70% have Ph.D. mostly from North America). The campus is spread over three and a half acres and is well-equipped with an amphitheater, a library, state-of-the-art laboratories, and more than 120 classrooms. The university is committed to research and global partnerships. This is why students are encouraged to engage in research projects, alongside conventional classroom-based learning. The Independent University has academic research collaborations with a number of prestigious universities including Harvard University, Stanford University, and the University of Colorado at Boulder.

Submission Guidelines

Final Paper Submission:

  • Prospective authors are invited to submit manuscripts reporting original unpublished research and recent developments in the topics related to the conference. It is required that the manuscript follows the standard IEEE format (IEEE standard double column, 10-point font).
  • Submitted paper should be in PDF format and should be of 4-8 pages
  • Submissions must include title, abstract, keywords, body and references.
  • Due to double-blind peer review requirement, paper must not contain author and affiliation information.
  • Download the IEEE templates in Microsoft Word and LaTeX format.
  • Authors are advised to upload full papers through Easy Chair conference management system using this link https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=sti20220

Camera-Ready Paper Submission Guidelines:

  • IEEE TEMPLATE:Please use the IEEE templates to prepare your camera-ready paper for STI-2022. Download the IEEE camera-ready templates for STI-2022 in Microsoft Word and LaTeX format.
  • AUTHORS NAME and AFFILIATIONS: Please add all authors with affiliations according to IEEE Template (Please use single block instead of n-blocks) for the conference and do not add page numbers.
  • FOOTER: Please add one IEEE Copyright Code according to the following conditions (usually it will be 978-1-6654-9045-0/22/$31.00 ©2022 IEEE) at the footer of the 1st page only (left column) of the paper.
    • For papers in which all authors are employed by the US government, the copyright notice is: U.S. Government work not protected by U.S. copyright.
